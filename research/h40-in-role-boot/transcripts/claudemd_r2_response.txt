⏺ Intent: Build a URL shortener service that maps short codes to destination URLs and redirects users.

  Clarifying questions

  Before decomposition is sound, I need answers to these:

  1. Scale — personal/internal tool, or public-facing with anonymous users and high redirect volume?
  2. Auth — do link creators need accounts, or is creation open/anonymous?
  3. Persistence — where do mappings live? (relational DB, KV store, in-memory for prototype?)
  4. Custom slugs — must users be able to specify their own short code, or is auto-generation sufficient?
  5. Analytics — should click counts, referrers, or geo data be tracked?
  6. Expiry — do links expire (by time or click count)?
  7. Deployment target — local/single-process, containerized, serverless, or hosted platform?
  8. Stack constraints — any language/framework requirements or preferences?
