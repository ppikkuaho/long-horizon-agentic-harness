⏺ Intent: Build a URL shortener service.

  Clarifying questions

  Before decomposition is sound, I need answers to these:

  1. Scale & traffic — Expected requests per day/month? Read-heavy (redirects) vs. write-heavy (link creation)?
  2. Auth & access control — Public link creation, or authenticated users only? Per-user link management (list, delete)?
  3. Custom slugs — Random-only (e.g. abc123) or user-defined vanity slugs (e.g. /my-sale)?
  4. Persistence — Links live forever, or do they expire? If expiry, time-based, click-count-based, or both?
  5. Analytics — Click counts only? Or geo, referrer, device breakdowns? Real-time or batch?
  6. Deployment target — Cloud (which provider?), self-hosted, serverless? Existing infra constraints?
  7. Existing systems — Greenfield, or must integrate with an existing auth/identity system?

  First-cut area decomposition

  │            Area             │                                Responsibility                                 │
